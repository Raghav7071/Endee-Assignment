Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB-PMJAY) is the world's largest health insurance scheme.
It was launched on 23 September 2018 and provides health coverage of Rs 5 lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization.
The scheme covers over 10 crore poor and vulnerable families across India.
AB-PMJAY is an entitlement-based scheme that identifies beneficiaries based on deprivation and occupational criteria from the SECC database.
The scheme covers medical and hospitalization expenses including pre and post hospitalization costs.
Treatment is available at all public hospitals and empanelled private hospitals across the country.
Beneficiaries can avail cashless and paperless treatment at the point of service.
The scheme covers about 1,929 medical procedures including surgeries, medical and day care treatments.
Ayushman Bharat also includes the creation of Health and Wellness Centres to provide comprehensive primary health care.
