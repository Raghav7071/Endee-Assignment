PM Kisan Samman Nidhi Yojana is a central government scheme launched in February 2019.
Under this scheme, eligible farmer families receive a financial benefit of Rs 6000 per year.
The amount is paid in three equal installments of Rs 2000 each directly into the bank accounts of the beneficiaries.
All landholding farmer families are eligible for this scheme, subject to certain exclusion criteria.
Institutional landholders, income tax payers, and constitutional post holders are excluded from the scheme.
As of 2024, over 11 crore farmers have benefited from PM Kisan across the country.
Farmers can register for PM Kisan through the official PM Kisan portal or by visiting their nearest Common Service Centre.
The scheme is fully funded by the Government of India and aims to supplement the financial needs of small and marginal farmers.
